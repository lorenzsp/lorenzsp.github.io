Outreach & Science Communication

I actively engage in public outreach and science communication to bridge research, art, and society. I presented gravitational wave science in German at the Potsdamer Tag der Wissenschaften, and regularly participate in initiatives that help refine my communication skills. Echoing Feynman, true understanding comes from the ability to explain complex concepts simply. I have given outreach talks at the TORCH Gallery in Amsterdam and the ESTEC Art Club.
